<div style="text-align: justify; text-justify: inter-word;">

I am a final-year PhD student in Operations Research in the Department of Industrial Engineering and Decision Analytics at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), where I am advised by Professor <a href="https://xiaoweiz.github.io/" target="_blank">Xiaowei Zhang</a>. 

My research lies at the intersection of simulation, machine learning, and data-driven decision-making under uncertainty. I am particularly interested in leveraging large language models and advanced statistical learning techniques to enhance modeling, optimization, and policy design in complex operational systems. Broadly, my work aims to develop interpretable and computationally efficient methodologies for improving decision quality in service operations, supply chain management, and financial risk management.

</div>
